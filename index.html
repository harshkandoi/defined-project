Defined.ai is ethical AI

The foundation of our company is based on the following principles:

  • Ethical data sourcing

    We source our data ethically, whether by crowd or by professional linguists.

  • Bias-aware ethos

    Our goal is to enable the AI builders of the future to create AI that is helpful to everyone, everywhere, no matter your race, accent, language, age, gender, or disability .

  • Risk-free data

    That meets all international privacy standards, such as GDPR.

  • Transparency

    Enables you to buy confidently and make ethically informed data decisions: proper age distribution balanced phonetics appropriate gender distribution dialectal detail, if applicable accent detail, if applicable model/use case application audio metadata detail
